Welcome!

Sex Trafficking is a global, national, Alaska and Kenai Peninsula form of Slavery.

Awareness expanded on the Kenai Peninsula after the Encounters of Hope dance performance at Kenai Central High School in February 2023. Hundreds watched this touching dance presentation expressing the pain and suffering of Sex Trafficking. Our community responded.

The result was the creation of an organization called: TRAFFICKED KNOW MORE
