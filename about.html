Our Philosophy and Vision

Philosophy
As a Christ centered organization, it is clear we need supernatural support and direction from God to fight the evil of Sex Trafficking. Our human condition brings weakness without God’s wisdom and courage. Prayer is needed from all Christians who are concerned with eradicating Sex Trafficking on the Kenai Peninsula. Too often individuals pray for self-centered goals.

1 John 5:14-15. “And this is the confidence that we have in him, that, if we ask any thing according to his will, he heareth us. And if we know that he hears us, whatsoever we ask, we know that we have the petitions that we desired of him.”
We believe God’s will is to stop Sex Trafficking if we step out in Faith and make the effort. 1 Corinthians 15:57-58.

Vision
We believe there are three components that can disrupt Sex Trafficking:

  1. Protect potential victims and help those suffering from the trauma of Sex Trafficking
  2. Decrease demand
  3. Decrease profitability for Traffickers
